Recording in the Studios

How to Start with Us Do you a song you want to record? Do you have an idea of a song? Do you just want a high-quality recording of yourself singing your favorite song? Do you want us to write you a hit song? If you said yes to any of these, we are the studio for you. We work with a wide range of clients on a wide range of projects. We have beginners and teens getting their first studio experience, we have Las Vegas musicians who have been in the game for years finishing their demos and we have o riginal artists making ready songs to get signed by major labels and we will be your guide and partner too! Just give us a call and our producers will gladly talk with you about your project and we will get you started and most importantly moving forward. Common Packages We customize everyones studio experience too fit their particular needs and goals. While this is true, if your confused where you want to begin here are three easy categories too choose from: 1) The Record Lab Experience - This is where you tell us a famous song you would like to sing or make your own and we make the track for you, record your voice on it and fix it up with all the studio magic to make it rival a star. This package is usually best for social media because covering famous songs will bring people to your page where they can find your other music. This is also a favorite of those looking to books gigs, first timers to a studio or as a birthday present. 2) Demo - The demo is for original songwriters and artists who want a fantastic recording either of an original song or an original cover (a famous song that is changed significantly). This is a less expensive production because it has everything it needs to be a beautiful song but does not go as crazy as a radio ready production. Examples of this can be a beautiful acoustic version of a famous song with lovely harmonies or a song with a full band (guitar, bass, drums, keys and voice) but does have two or three back up singers and horns. Demos are usually used for social media or to sell a song or artist to a label. 3) Radio Ready - If you are trying to be a famous artist this is the one for you. This is where all the magic is mixed together to make a masterpiece. Radio Ready refers to a song that is made to such high quality that it is ready to go on the radio today and go up against all the big names.

The Team

Meet Steve Stephen Noel Tracy, also known as Steve, cut his teeth back in the early 80s when he worked with Curb recorders. At this time, he worked hand in hand with the players of the age getting bands on early MTV, running stage commands for famous artist such as Rick Springfield and working on the music side of movies. He also holds a Masters degree in Psychology, which helps him understand and work with clients on an amazing level. “No one can work a room like Steve, if you need someone to move your product this is the guy.” - Howie Rosen (former A&R) Meet Stephentodd Stephen Todd Tracy, who goes by Stephentodd, began at the age of 14 doing gigs around Las Vegas. It was through this work and love of music he decided to make music his life. After Leaving Brigham Young University in 2013, he returned home to Las Vegas to work with Mr. Martin a multi platinum producer. Through this internship he learned how to understand a market, find a demographic and most importantly produce a hit song. He often says, “I was blessed to work with such large artist at such a young age.” After that he went to work at another multiplatinum studio before opening his own with his father. Since opening his own studio Stephentodd has won multiple awards and had multiple songs break the top 40 on the billboard charts.
