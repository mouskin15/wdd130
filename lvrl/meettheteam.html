Meeting the Team

Meet Stephen Todd Tracy, who goes by Stephentodd, began at the age of 14 doing gigs around Las Vegas. It was through this work and love of music he decided to make music his life. After Leaving Brigham Young University in 2013, he returned home to Las Vegas to work with Mr. Martin a multi platinum producer. Through this internship he learned how to understand a market, find a demographic and most importantly produce a hit song. He often says, “I was blessed to work with such large artist at such a young age.” After that he went to work at another multiplatinum studio before opening his own with his father. Since opening his own studio Stephentodd has won multiple awards and had multiple songs break the top 40 on the billboard charts.

Meet Stephen Noel Tracy, also known as Steve, cut his teeth back in the early 80s when he worked with Curb recorders. At this time, he worked hand in hand with the players of the age getting bands on early MTV, running stage commands for famous artist such as Rick Springfield and working on the music side of movies. He also holds a Masters degree in Psychology, which helps him understand and work with clients on an amazing level. “No one can work a room like Steve, if you need someone to move your product this is the guy.” - Howie Rosen (former A&R)

About the team members

The Owners of Las Vegas Record Lab LLC was founded in the year two thousand and fourteen by a father and son duo named Stephen Noel Tracy, the father, and Stephen Todd Tracy, the son. Between the two of them they have worked in studios all around the country. They have produced tracks that have cracked the Billboard Top 40, worked along some of the worlds biggest artists and performed around the world.
