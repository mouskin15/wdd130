Recording in the Studios

Recording in the Studios A Business Focused Space Las Vegas Record Lab is a personal boutique studio hand built by Steve for the highest quality. It runs off of the top-of-the-line industry standard equipment. The DAW is Protools 12. If you have worked before but not on Protools never fear, we can happily convert any of your sessions in a Protools file for you. We pride ourselves in our affordable nature as we keep the focus on the creation and marketing of music and not on huge spaces that are mainly used for show and not for function. Its through this focus we have been able to compete with the “big boys” in Las Vegas (and even beat them!) while keeping our costs often one half of theirs.
